EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Daniel’s Business center is a start-up business that will sell provision materials or products. The proposed business will be located in the Lelekpayea community especially the Boy’s Town area. The business will start-up with the purchase of some provision product that will be resold on market with different prices. Daniel’s business center will make it possible for everyone to meet their needs and want within the community and create more interest in purchasing my product. The objective of establishing this proposed business center especially Boy’s town is based on the following: To present different kind of product to the people of Boy’s town and by crafting different style that appeal to my potential customer. On the overall, the objective is to meet the people basic needs and wants in the Lelekpayeh community. Before thinking about entering into this business a research was conducted beginning the 3rd, of April to the 28 of May. During my research I took into consideration the total number of people that will use my business path whether they will need my product, who my potential customer will be, how often will they demand my product or services within the same research I was able to identify my competitor weakness and strength which strategies in promoting and pricing my goods. The start-up budget for this proposed business is in the sum of USD$750. From this amount the total cost of my start-up asset is $377.683USD while the total cost of my start-up inventory is USD $372.317 and total the amount of $750USD.

Based on my entrepreneur experience, I will price my products taking into consideration my expected profit and forget the overcharging a particularly to avoid the over stay of goods.

My customer will be less by 15% discount based on the quality of goods purchased and services need.

My price will be posted under every product on the shelf. To have a good link and boost of my business, my potential customer will be credited with proper record system when there is need.
